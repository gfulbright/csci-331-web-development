Phineas and the Ferb-Tones Fan Webpage

Phineas and the Ferb-Tones is a 2000's pop band with members Phineas Flynn, Ferb Fletcher, Isabella Garcia-Shapiro, the Fireside Girls, and feature guest appearance from Candace Flynn.

The group's motivation was to write a catchy tune with meaningless lyrics to become a successful pop song.

They followed the popstar checklist to become a one-hit wonder:

  • Write a hit record
  • Melt down in a diva tantrum
  • Have your music reduced to elevator music
  • Become popular once again in a reunion tour
